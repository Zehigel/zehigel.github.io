Heya! I’m Olli, ui/ux designer, illustrator and graphic designer based in the lovely Irenental in Austria.

For the last couple of years, I’ve been focusing on improving my skills in various scopes of design trying to dig deeper into several directions to find out what fits best for me. Doing that, at some point I realized that once I started finding those fields I wanna work in, the search itself is exactly what fulfills me. Not to focus just on one scope, but to focus on all of them and improve wherever I can, reaching out to many companies from all over the world, have learnings from various collaborations, that’s what is pure delight for me.
Living in Austria with my partner and photography genius Johanna, I got the perfect contrast to my daily business. I love to stay in the woods, travelling around with my friends as well as playing boardgames with my familiy and enjoy a beauteous living together.
